Dunkirk
2017 | 12 | 106 minutes

Allied soldiers are surrounded by German forces and trapped on the beaches of Dunkirk during World War II. The film follows three separate storylines: one on land, one at sea, and one in the air.
Over the course of a week, the soldiers desperately try to evacuate the beach, while civilians in small boats attempt to rescue them. The film is a stunning tribute to the bravery and resilience of those involved in the Dunkirk evacuation.

Director Christopher Nolan

Writer Christopher Nolan

Starring Fionn Whitehead | Barry Keoghan | Tom Hardy | Cillian Murphy
